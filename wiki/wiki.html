About Expertiza

Expertiza is an open source project based on Ruby on Rails framework. Expertiza allows instructors to create new assignments and customize new or existing assignments. It also allows instructors to create a list of topics that students can sign up for. Students can form teams in Expertiza to work on various projects and assignments. Students can also peer review other students' submissions. Expertiza supports submission across various document types, including URLs and wiki pages.

About TeamsParticipant Model

The `TeamsParticipant` model manages the relationship between teams and participants in Expertiza. This model replaces the older `TeamsUser` model to provide a more structured way of handling team memberships. The key differences are:

  • Direct association with participants instead of users
  • Better separation of concerns between team membership and user management
  • Improved data integrity through proper relationships
  • More efficient querying of team memberships
  • Better handling of team member duties and roles
  • Enhanced error handling and validation

New Implementation

Model Changes

class TeamsParticipant < ApplicationRecord
  belongs_to :team
  belongs_to :participant
  validates :team_id, presence: true
  validates :participant_id, presence: true
end
